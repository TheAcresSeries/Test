The Trilogy

Three Books.
One Unforgettable Journey.

I

Book One

Acres

Gettysburg, Pennsylvania

Alicia is a sharp-witted teenager with an extraordinary gift: she can slip inside the consciousness of anyone — living or long dead. From her treehouse on the acres, she mind-travels into Russell and Abigail, two siblings navigating life in 19th-century Gettysburg. Between past-life visits, she navigates family mayhem, first love, and the mystery of who vandalized the school windows with Poe.

II

Book Two

Scar

Savannah, Georgia

The family road trips eleven hours South to Savannah. Alicia's mind-travel abilities deepen as she explores the city's haunted past and discovers the ghost of Emma Lou Riley — known by the daisies always tucked into her hair — wandering cousin Daphne's historic home. Meanwhile, Michael and his friends face the consequences of their Poe-inspired school prank in a York County courtroom.

Learn More →
III

Book Three

Arc

Gettysburg, Pennsylvania

Back on the acres for the Fourth of July, Alicia reunites with Daphne as summer reaches its peak. The visits to Russell and Abigail turn dangerous — leading to a breathtaking climax in an underground crystal cave where Russell's courage is tested beyond its limit. When the final page turns, the boundary between past and present may never be the same again.

The Cast

Characters

A

Alicia

Narrator · Mind-Traveler

Sharp, sarcastic, and perceptive. Her extraordinary ability to enter other people's consciousness makes her witness to history — whether she wants to be or not.

R

Russell

19th Century · The Acres

A stubborn, funny boy who can't spell his own name but would walk into the earth itself to protect his sister. Alicia's favorite mind-travel host.

Ab

Abigail

19th Century · The Acres

Russell's quick-tongued younger sister. Smarter than anyone gives her credit for, and brave far beyond what her era would expect of her.

M

Mason

Alicia's Older Brother

Cool, unbothered, and master of the staring contest. He has exactly two modes: unbothered and winning.

P

Patrick

Alicia's Younger Brother

Enthusiastic, perpetually hungry, and a born negotiator who can extract concessions from his mother with alarming efficiency.

Mi

Michael

Love Interest

Thoughtful and quietly romantic. He wrote Edgar Allan Poe across the school windows — all for Alicia — and faced the consequences with dignity.

D

Daphne

Cousin · Savannah, GA

Fearless, hilarious, and an eager partner in ghost-hunting. She lives in a haunted house and treats it as a perk rather than a problem.

E

Emma "Daisy" Riley

Ghost · Savannah

Identified by the daisies always tucked into her hair. She wanders Daphne's historic Savannah home — and Alicia promises to plant seeds for her in the North too.

About the Author

      
Shane Michael Quaker

      

      
Shane Michael Quaker is the author of The Acres Series, a coming-of-age trilogy set in Gettysburg, Pennsylvania and Savannah, Georgia. The series follows Alicia, a teenager with the rare ability to mind-travel through time and consciousness, as she uncovers history, family secrets, and the mysteries of the acres she calls home.
